## Your Privacy Rights

For individuals located in the European Economic Area (EEA), Netspire Dev processes personal data in full compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

Under GDPR, you have specific rights regarding how your personal information is collected, used, and stored.

These rights include:

- **Right of Access**
  You may request confirmation of whether we process your personal data and obtain a copy of that information, along with details on how it is used.

- **Right to Rectification**  
  You may request correction of inaccurate, incomplete, or outdated personal information.

- **Right to Erasure (Right to Be Forgotten)**
  You may request deletion of your personal data where lawful grounds apply.

- **Right to Restrict Processing**
  You may request a temporary limitation on processing in specific situations, such as when accuracy or legality is under review.

- **Right to Object**
  You may object to processing carried out on legitimate interest grounds, including profiling or direct communication activities.

- **Right to Data Portability**
  You may request a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable copy of your personal data for transfer to another service provider.
